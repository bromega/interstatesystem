It may seem weird, but U.S. Highways also play a part in Interstates. First of all, If a U.S. Highway has a specific number, Interstates can’t have it. So that’s why there’s no Interstate 12, 23, 24, 31, 66, etc. If an Interstate has a weird number, the reason is probably because a U.S. Highway has it.

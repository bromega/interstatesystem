One weird Interstate is I-69 Because it differs from most Interstates(see chapter 7). It goes North-South just like all other Interstates with an odd first number. But after the junction with I-96 in Michigan which then says I-69 East towards Flint, which is totally bonkers because it ends in a 9! I-69 goes from the Mexican border as I-69 E, W, C, to the Canadian border with the last junction being with I-94 in Port Huron.
